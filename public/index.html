Your dashboard

Everything from every call, in one place.

Full visibility into every conversation your AI handles — calls, leads, jobs, quotes, and calendar.

01 / CALLS

Call history & transcripts

Every call logged with a full AI transcript, recording, duration, and caller details. Click into any call to see the complete conversation.

02 / LEADS

Lead pipeline

Every caller becomes a lead. Track status from new to booked to won. See urgency, source, and AI confidence at a glance.

03 / QUOTES

AI-drafted quotes

After each call, Diali matches transcript details against your pricebook to draft a quote. You review it, adjust if needed, and send.

04 / JOBS

Job board

Track every job from booking to completion. Board view or list view with checklists, parts, and notes for each job.

05 / CALENDAR

Appointment calendar

See all your booked appointments in a clean calendar view. Click into any day to see what's scheduled and who's coming.

06 / AI AGENT

Fully configurable

Choose your voice, customize the greeting, set qualification questions, upload your pricebook. Test call your AI right from the browser.
